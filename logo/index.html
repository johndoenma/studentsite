jaipur city logo

Designing a logo for an international city that embodies its spirit, arts, culture, history, and present.

Overview

Jaipur is one of India's most culturally rich heritage cities. The floral piece represents Jaipur's history of royalty, hospitality, and culture. In 1876, Maharaja Ram Singh painted all royal and official buildings pink to symbolize hospitality, which is why “The Pink City” has become so famous. Today, the city is known for its bazaars, forts, palaces, temples, and wildlife sanctuaries.
